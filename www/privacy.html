Privacy policy agreement for application Remote Televizor




Privacy of its users is for android application Remote Televizor extremely important. All processed data are considered strictly confidential and are handled in accordance with the law.

What is purpose of application?

Remote Televizor represents remote control of another Android applications like Online Televizor

What informations are collected?

Application collects only such data, that is necessary to communicate with another devices through TCP-IP: IP address and port. This data is not transferred anywhere and the author of the application does not have access to it.
